Dress for School- Dress for Success is an international not-for-profit organization that empowers women to achieve economic independence by providing a network of support, professional attire and the development tools to help women thrive in work and in life
This organization arranges pick up for clothes that you may not want or wear to donate for the less fortunate. The organization provides pickups in major states in the U.S. such as Georgia, Florida, California and many more.
The Blue Jeans Go Green program collects denim across the country and upcycles it into UltraTouch Denim Insulation, providing a portion to communities in need each year.
The Salvation Army, an international movement, is an evangelical part of the universal Christian Church. Its message is based on the Bible. Its ministry is motivated by the love of God. Its mission is to preach the gospel of Jesus Christ and to meet human needs in His name without discrimination.
